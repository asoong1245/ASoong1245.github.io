Arthur Soong CRAP website evaluations

CRAP design evaluation

NASA

  • Contrast: Good contrast between text and background, images are also used to draw the user's attention.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of colors, fonts, and page layout across the entire site.
  • Alignment: Alignment is well structured and makes use of a grid pattern to organize the site.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together, this makes it easy of users to find what they're looking for.

Yale School of Art

  • Contrast: Contrast with very bright colors and a cluttered background.
  • Repetition: Very little repetition in layout, making each page feel disconnected from the others.
  • Alignment: Very bad alignment, content on the page is arranged in uneven sections.
  • Proximity: Related information is separated and unrelated elements are grouped together making things confusing.
